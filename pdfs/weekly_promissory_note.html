SUMMARY OF TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF LOAN TRANSACTION

For and in consideration of the loan granted to me, I agree to the following:

  1. I hereby authorize (a) my Employer to debit or withdraw an amount representing my Loan payment from my designated employee payroll account on behalf of Advance, (b) my Employer to transfer or remit the said debited or deducted amount to Advance; and (c) for Advance to receive each payment due on each due date. All payments are to be applied first to the payment of the accrued interest, then to the late payment penalty and late payment charges, when applicable, and lastly, to the balance of outstanding Principal.

  2. Should I fail to pay the Principal of this Note at maturity or on demand, as the case may be, or any installments thereof or fail to pay any other obligation which I now or in the future owe to the Lender, for any reason whatsoever, then the entire Principal of this Note, including any accrued interests and other charges or penalties due on this Note (the "Outstanding Amount"), shall, at the option of the Lender and without necessity of notice to me, immediately become due and payable.

    Events of Default - Upon the happening of any of the following events, the Lender may at its option, accelerate the maturity of this Note, and the entire Outstanding Amount of this Note shall automatically become due and payable on the immediate pay cycle without demand or notice, likewise without further notice:

    1. any interest or installment is not paid fully on due date;
    2. the Borrower dies or becomes insolvent (i.e., could no longer pay the undersigned Borrower’s obligations as they fall due) or any proceeding for dissolution, insolvency, suspension of payment, receivership, voting trust, foreclosure, attachment, garnishment, execution or similar act, is filed or commenced against the undersigned Borrower/s;
    3. in case of conviction for a criminal offense with final judgement carrying with it the penalty of civil interdiction affecting the undersigned Borrower;
    4. in case of resignation, termination or severance from employment of the Borrower from his/her employer;
    5. in case any collateral promised or given as security for this Note is not delivered in full, or is lost, destroyed or depreciates in value or cannot be immediately enforced;
    6. in case any provision of the agreement covering the collateral promised or given as security for the payment of this Note is violated or not complied with;
    7. upon the occurrence of any act or event which in the Lender's opinion, results in the impairment of the financial capacity of the Borrower;
    8. in case any representation or warranty, covenant or undertaking embodied in this Note, in the Corporate Salary Loan Application Form, or in other documents given by me in connection with the granting of the Loan turns out to be untrue or ceases to be true in any material respect, or is violated or not complied.
  3. In case of any change in my payday schedule implemented by my employer, I hereby authorize the Lender to alter or modify the Loan Payment Schedule to coincide with my new payday schedule and hereby consent to such adjustment. Prior written notice on the adjustment of the Loan Payment Schedule will be given by the Lender, to take effect on the date indicated therein.
  4. I agree that any amount advanced by the Lender in relation to this Note shall become part of the obligation due under this Note and non-payment thereof shall render the whole balance of the Note due and payable. This may be waived at the sole discretion of the Lender.
  5. I hereby agree to pay a late payment penalty of two and a half percent (2.5%) weekly interest for missed or partial payment on the Outstanding Amount due until it has been fully repaid. This penalty shall accrue from date of default and may only be waived at the sole discretion of the Lender. The Lender has the sole discretion to refer the account to a third-party collection agency and the credit bureau beginning fifteen (15) days after the first day of missed payment, or once the account has been flagged as delinquent. All other expenses incurred due to my late payment, such as bank charges, shall be for my account. The said late payment penalty and penalty interest imposed on me is immediately due and payable, and may be collected by the Lender through any of the available payment channels as listed on the Lender’s website.
  6. I likewise hereby agree to pay the total amount incurred by the Lender in connection with the collection of any amount due from me under this Note, including the attorney’s fees, litigation expenses and costs of suit. In addition to the foregoing costs and charges, I further agree to pay by way of liquidated damages, the following amounts:

    • ten percent (10%) of the total amount due, including Principal, interest and charges, in case no suit is filed; or

    • twenty-five percent (25%) of total amount due, including Principal, interest and charges, in case a collection suit is filed in court.
  7. Any action to enforce payment of this Note may be brought by the Lender, at its sole option, in the regular courts of Mandaluyong City or in any city or province where the Lender has a branch office, at the option of the Lender.
  8. This Note and related contracts between the parties shall be governed by Philippine law.
  9. I shall not assign, transfer or permit the assignment or transfer of any of the rights or obligations under this Note without the prior written consent of the Lender.
  10. I acknowledge that my current employment is a primary consideration for Advance to extend the loan in my favor, without security and other requirements, and I undertake to inform Advance of any changes to my employment status within twenty-four (24) hours from the time such intention is communicated to the employer. Failure to do so may result in the account being tagged as delinquent.
  11. I further acknowledge that in extending the loan, Advance relied on the personal information I provided through my employer or directly to Advance. In the event that Advance finds out that I knowingly or fraudulently provided any erroneous personal information, then I agree that the loan will become immediately due and payable upon notice to me. I also agree that Advance reserves the right to file an action for damages against me.
  12. I agree that the entire outstanding Principal, including interest, will be deducted from my final salary upon resignation or termination from my present employer. In the event that the outstanding balance due under this Note is not fully repaid when I receive my final salary from my present employer, I agree that (a) my loan will be declared delinquent, and (b) I will pay the two and a half percent (2.5%) weekly penalty interest until the outstanding balance has been fully repaid.
  13. If any provision of this agreement shall be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall not be affected, thereby and every provision hereof, shall be valid and enforceable to the fullest extent permitted by law.
